About us



Who Are Butwal Coder Girls

Butwal Coder Girls is an initiative brought with an aim of sustainably and trategically increasing the number of women in technology by securing meaningful tech work for girls. Butwal Coder Girls want to educate young, talented and bright girls and lead them to become the type of women the industry looks. Through this initiative equip girls with critical technology skills that will prepare them for a career in the fields of technology. Girls will learn how to give preparesentation, how to design website, how to make application and many more.

It is a organization which focuses to boost up the girls in IT sector. Nowadays, new inventions are occurring every day in technology and IT has become the habitual to the people but most Nepalese woman’s/girls are unreachable to the IT sector. So, Butwal Coder Girls is aiming to collaborate with all the IT passionate girls and give new shape to this place in IT Sector. It is continuously conducting events, programs and different competition related to IT field. It also conducts motivating and counseling programs from different personals.

In our organization, all members are IT related, so it will be the great chance to build a strong bond with the people and experts in your field. It also provides the practical experience in the safe environment. We provide a platform to share your skills and it can also be helpful in gaining leadership skills and you’ll be presented with opportunities to improve in public speaking and gain confidence in yourself as an individual. In future, Butwal Coder Girls is planning to aware the girls about IT field from school level. So, this will be the great opportunity to give back to the community, through the act of service or by sponsoring events. This organization appreciates the new ideas, you can generate new ideas. Joining Butwal Coder Girls will be helpful to push your career to the next level, broaden your skills and reach in IT field. Another, exciting thing about Butwal Coder Girls is it can also be workplace of the future.
