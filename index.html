Butwal Coder Girls



About us

Butwal Coder Girls is an initiative brought with an aim of sustainably and trategically increasing the number of women in technology by securing meaningful tech work for girls.

Butwal Coder Girls want to educate young, talented and bright girls and lead them to become the type of women the industry looks. Through this initiative equip girls with critical technology skills that will prepare them for a career in the fields of technology. Girls will learn how to give preparesentation, how to design website, how to make application and many more.

Objective

    Innovation

We are highly focus to encourage innovative, creative and great ideas of girls.

 Education

We organize various seminar, events and training to educate women in the field of technology.

  Increment

We are working on to increase number of girls in tech field by providing empowerment programs.
